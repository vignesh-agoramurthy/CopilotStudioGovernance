# Observability

**Welcome to Observability for Copilot Studio.**

Observability is critical for understanding, monitoring, and optimizing the performance of your AI agents in production. This section provides comprehensive guidance on monitoring, logging, analytics, and troubleshooting your Copilot Studio agents.

## Overview

Discover how to achieve complete visibility into your agent operations:

- **Monitoring & Metrics**: Track agent performance, usage patterns, and operational health
- **Logging & Diagnostics**: Capture and analyze detailed execution logs and error traces
- **Analytics & Insights**: Measure success metrics and identify optimization opportunities
- **Troubleshooting**: Debug issues and resolve common problems efficiently
- **Alerting & Notifications**: Set up proactive alerts for critical issues

> [!NOTE]
> Observability enables you to move from reactive troubleshooting to proactive monitoring and continuous improvement of your agent implementations.
